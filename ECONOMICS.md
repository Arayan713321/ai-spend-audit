# ECONOMICS.md (Unit Economics)

## Lead Value to Credex
A "Converted Lead" for Credex is a company that moves their AI spend to Credex-sourced credits.
- **Avg. Monthly Spend**: $2,000.
- **Credex Margin**: 10–15% (via arbitrage or platform fees).
- **Monthly Revenue per Lead**: ~$250.
- **LTV (12-month retention)**: **$3,000**.
- **Upsell Potential**: Moving them to Enterprise credits (Cursor, OpenAI) increases LTV to **$10k+**.

## Channel CAC (Estimates)
- **Organic Social (X/HN)**: $0 (Time-intensive).
- **Newsletter Sponsorships (AI/Dev focused)**: $200 - $500 per placement.
- **Total CAC Goal**: < $150 per lead.

## Profitability Threshold
- **Audit → Consultation Booking Rate**: 3%
- **Booking → Credit Purchase Rate**: 20%
- **Total Audit-to-Sale Conversion**: 0.6%
- **Requirement**: For every 1,000 audits, we need 6 sales to generate $18,000 LTV. 
- **Breakeven**: If CAC is $150, we can afford to spend $900 per 1,000 audits.

## Road to $1M ARR
To hit $1M ARR (Annual Recurring Revenue) in 18 months:
1. **Audits per month**: 20,000.
2. **Conversions (0.6%)**: 120 customers/month.
3. **Avg Customer Revenue**: $250/mo.
4. **Monthly Added Rev**: $30,000.
5. **Month 18 ARR**: ~$1.08M (assuming low churn).

**Key Assumption**: The viral nature of the shareable audit links reduces the marginal CAC to near zero over time, allowing for aggressive scaling without linear spend.
